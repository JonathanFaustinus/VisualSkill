About VisualSkill

We started with one oven, one camera, and a belief that anyone anywhere can learn to bake beautifully.

Our Story

From a Small Kitchen to a Global Platform

VisualSkill was founded in 2020 by a small team of pastry chefs in Jakarta, Indonesia, who shared a frustration existing baking content online was either too vague for beginners or too expensive to access.

Starting with just three courses filmed in a home kitchen, VisualSkill grew rapidly as students discovered that visual, step-by-step baking education genuinely worked. Within a year, we had 10,000 registered learners.

We are proud to be a platform built by bakers, for bakers where technique meets accessibility, and passion meets craft.

Our Purpose

Vision and Mision

Our Vision

To become the world's most trusted and beloved bakery education platform where every passionate baker, regardless of background or location, has access to professional-grade culinary knowledge.

We envision a world where the smell of freshly baked bread bridges cultures, and the act of baking brings joy and confidence to every kitchen.

Our Mission

To create the most visual, accessible, and comprehensive bakery courses on the internet taught by real professionals, structured for genuine learning, and priced for everyone.

We are committed to making professional baking education available to anyone with a kitchen and a dream, removing the barrier of geography, cost, and language.
